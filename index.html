Hi there. At some point in 2019 I took down my site because it hadn't been updated in 5+ years. I'm still working on the replacement at a rate of about 1 byte/month, so in the meantime here's the barebones version.

My name is Tobias. I'm a design leader with an engineering background. I grew up in the Danish countryside but these days I'm based in San Francisco.

I'm somewhat obsessed with 80's pen plotters. I wrote them a love letter in the form of a tutorial on Medium. For some reason it blew up on Hacker News last year.

I'm currently a Creative Director at Google. Before that I spent time at Pinterest and at the design firm IDEO.
